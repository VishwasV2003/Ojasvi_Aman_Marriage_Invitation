The home that held her heart

Her world was shaped in the warmth of Ganpat Lal Kothari and Pratibha Vijayvargiya—a father whose steadiness became her courage, and a mother whose grace taught her how to love without measure. Their blessings rest beneath every step she takes toward the mandap.

The home that shaped his path

He carries forward the legacy of Lt. Mahesh Kumar Gupta and Damyanti Gupta—duty worn lightly, kindness spoken often, and a home where honour was lived rather than merely taught. We seek your affection for them as they give their son to this new beginning.
